Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

What is the scope of your research?

+

Our research focuses on artificial intelligence applications in defense contexts, specifically examining operational effectiveness, strategic implications, and implementation challenges for Saudi Arabia and the broader Gulf region.

Do you conduct classified research?

+

We conduct unclassified research and analysis. We do not accept classified material through public channels and operate as an independent research resource for educational and analytical purposes.

How is your research funded?

+

We operate as an independent research organization funded through academic partnerships, industry collaboration, and thought leadership initiatives. Our independence ensures objective analysis and recommendations.

Collaboration Process

What types of collaboration do you offer?

+

We offer research collaboration, advisory services, technology assessment, strategic planning support, and regional partnership development. Collaboration scope varies from consultation projects to long-term strategic partnerships.

How long do collaboration projects typically last?

+

Collaboration duration varies by scope. Advisory consultations typically span 3-6 months, research collaborations extend 6-18 months, and strategic partnerships are long-term engagements spanning several years.

Do you work with organizations outside Saudi Arabia?

+

Yes, we collaborate with defense organizations, research institutions, and technology companies globally. Our focus remains on applications relevant to Middle Eastern security contexts while leveraging international expertise.

Technical Capabilities

What technical disciplines do you cover?

+

Our technical expertise spans artificial intelligence, machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, autonomous systems, cybersecurity, predictive analytics, and defense systems integration.

Do you provide software development services?

+

We focus on research, analysis, and strategy rather than software development. However, we can provide technical guidance, algorithm development assistance, and integration consulting for defense AI applications.

What are your data security protocols?

+

We maintain rigorous data security protocols including encrypted communications, secure data storage, access controls, and regular security assessments. All collaboration agreements include detailed confidentiality and security provisions.
